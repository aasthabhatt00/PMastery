Becoming a Great Product Manager

Understanding Product Management

Product Management is an organizational function that guides every step of a product's lifecycle. The role of a Product Manager includes defining the 'why', 'when', and 'what' of the product that the engineering team builds.

Types of Product Management

  • Product Development Management
  • Product Marketing Management
  • Technical Product Management
  • Data Product Management

Steps to Becoming a Great Product Manager

  1. Educational Background
  2. Gain Relevant Experience
  3. Develop Essential Skills
  4. Understand the Tools and Technologies
  5. Certification and Continuous Learning
  6. Build a Network
  7. Gain Mastery Over Time

Advanced Strategies for Experienced PMs

  • Market Positioning and Competitive Analysis
  • Customer Development
  • Product Roadmapping
